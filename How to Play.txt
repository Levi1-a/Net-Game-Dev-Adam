How to Play
- Run NewNetPro2.0.exe
- Enter Name and click Host or Client
- Click start when all players are ready

The unbuilt Unity version is in the folder titled "Unbuilt Unity Code"

Controls
•	WASD – move
•	Mouse – aim
•	Left click – shoot
•	Shift – sprint
•	Space – jump
•	U – open the scorecard
•	I – close the scorecard
